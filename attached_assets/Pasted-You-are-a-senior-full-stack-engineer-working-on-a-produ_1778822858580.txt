# PICKUP OPTIMIZATION ALGORITHM

DO NOT use simple midpoint averaging.

Instead:

## STEP 1

Cluster rider coordinates.

Calculate:

* rider average location
* rider spread radius

---

## STEP 2

Evaluate all Walmart/Target locations.

Only consider locations:

* near the driver route corridor
* within reasonable detour range

---

## STEP 3

For each candidate location calculate:

score =
(driver_detour_minutes * 0.7)
+
(avg_rider_travel_minutes * 0.3)

Choose:

* lowest scoring location
